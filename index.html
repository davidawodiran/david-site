About Velzo

What: A marketplace for digital products (ebooks, courses, templates, software) with built-in discovery, buyers browse, search, and get recommendations.

Who: Creators and small businesses in Africa who have great products but no audience or distribution.

The Problem: Platforms like Selar and Sellfy are just storefronts. Creators must bring 100% of their own traffic. Most never make their first sale.

Our Solution: Built-in discovery (buyers browse and find products organically) + Africa-first payments (reliable local payouts).
